title: Starting 2026 The Way We Mean To Go On

So its already February, and the end of it at that. It doesn't seem like missing two or three days from a month should make all that much of a difference, but it really does. Well, for me, this year it's feeling like it. We've got a whole week left as of me writing this...but that doesn't feel like a lot.

Not that I really have anything major coming up in March, other than that normal march forward we're always dealing with.

What does that have to do with this blog and site? Not all that much, other than 'that march forward' is often on my mind as I work on my things. My day job, my personal life, this website, my novel, other writing, art projects, DnD game....I could keep going, but you get the idea. I know more than one of you gets up to the same project heavy shenanigans that I do so I won't apologize.

I stopped trying to change that habit, that routine, years ago. It just felt like fighting a current, instead of working with it. I am not a person who works well with hyper focus over long periods of time. Its hard to try, almost impossible to keep up, and makes me miserable. Anyone around me suffers the consequences, and as someone who is frequently around me I don't want to deal with those consequences. So- going with the flow!

This is how I mean to go into 2026- going with my flow, even as the occasional rapids pick up and turn things over. Does that mean I don't have any concrete updates for you? Not at all.

Discerning eyes might have already noticed that there is a "shop" link on the site now. It links to the Print-On-Demand that I've had for along time, but was never connected to here. If you've never explored there before give it a poke. There are some of my favorite artworks on there available for printing on things like shirts and notebooks.

I have some new art works that I have been making as always, some just for myself, others that I would like to share, but I think I want to edit how things are shared on here before I invest the time to upload a lot. The journey of learning how ti run and design a website also marches forward!

For those of you keeping track- I am still working on Feathers and Flowers. I won't go into detail but I've learned a lot about the setting and time frame, characters and arcs that I want to explore. I also thing I have a much better idea of the tone of the story as I go into a further draft. I ended 2025 feeling a little down about the project, but I've found the passion again, so well, that adds a steam engine to my travel down stream. At least for a while.

Anything else...well, I'm certain there is, but maybe not in the purview of this site.

I will leave you with this question: What are you starting now that you would like to take into the rest of 2026?
